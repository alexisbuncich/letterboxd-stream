How it works
I
Open your list
Navigate to any public Letterboxd list in your browser.
II
View source
Press Ctrl+U (or ⌘⌥U on Mac) to open the raw HTML source.
III
Copy & paste
Ctrl+A then Ctrl+C to copy. Use + Add Page for multi-page lists — films are deduplicated automatically.
IV
Check streams
Select your region and hit Find Films. We'll check every major streaming service.
